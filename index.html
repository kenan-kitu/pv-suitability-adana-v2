PR is usually ~0.70–0.85. EF and household demand are assumptions; we’ll justify later in report.

2) Suitability scenario

Coverage means: “What % of the available land will actually be covered by PV arrays?” (roads, spacing, slope constraints, etc. reduce coverage)
Coverage: 10%
These SR values are placeholders for testing. Later you will replace them with zonal statistics from your ArcGIS Solar Radiation outputs.

3) Area input

Option A: Draw polygon → we compute its area.
Option B: Use preset “available suitable area” values (temporary for testing).
Formula: Energy (kWh/yr) = SR (kWh/m²/yr) × Area (m²) × Coverage × Efficiency × PR
